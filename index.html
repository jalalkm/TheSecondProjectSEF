Tartous is a city located on the Mediterranean coast of Syria. It is the second largest coastal city in Syria (after Latakia), and the largest city in Tartus Governorate. Until the 1970s, Tartous was part of Latakia Governorate, then it became a separate governorate. The population is 458,327 people (2023 estimate). In the summer, it is a vacation spot for many Syrians. Many holiday complexes and resorts are located in the area. Many hotels of all classes and various restaurants. It includes a commercial port.
